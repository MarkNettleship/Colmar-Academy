Reimagine urban

Our latest colleciton focuses on the work of our recent graduates, who worked really hard to create this weird, blocky building. The main point of this building is that it is made purely of gases. The airflow is constructed in such a way

as to encourage gases to build up in more dense patterns where walls are required. The walls block all wind and cold from outside, but can be walked through from inside to outside, making the doors strictly decorative.
